Our Students

Thabane high school was established in 1979. The school is an ordinary public school situated in Kimberley in the Northern Cape, South Africa. Currently it consists of 1 principla,1 deputy principal 4 heads of departments 21 teachers. The school offers Grade 8 up to Grade 12. We have about 811 leaners in total 27 teachers in total 13 mails and 14 femails. Our dressing code in uniform is Marron and White, marron socks and black shoes. Boys ware grey trousers marron or gray socks, white shirts marron tie with white stripes. Girls marron skirts white blouses, marron tie with white stripes.

Our Vision

Ensuring that every learner excels at school and leaves Thabane Secondary School with skills and knowledge that will give them the best chance of success in future.

Mission

This school was established to enable all young people who enrol here to build together a future where each respects the worth and dignity of the other and accepts a mutual responsibility for creating an ethos where teaching and learning can flourish and where each one can fully develop his or her potential. Together, we will strive to build a school where young minds, spirits and bodies, rooted in sound moral values and an acceptance of our common humanity under God, can be nurtured and encouraged to reach new frontiers.
